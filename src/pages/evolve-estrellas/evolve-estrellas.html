As rock ‘n’ roll declines, Imagine Dragons continue to ascend.

Thanks to a monster 2017, in which the Las Vegas quartet had the best-selling rock album of the year with their third release, Evolve, and the No. 1 and No. 2 top rock singles with their songs Believer and Thunder, Imagine Dragons have entered 2018 as America’s most popular rock band.
